A DNA sequence is a string of ATCG characters representing possible nucleotide base. The complement of a DNA sequence is obtained by substituting each base with its base pair (A and T are one pair. C and G is the other). Together the two strands containing the sequence form a DNA molecule. A restriction enzyme cuts off DNA strands at a specific binding site in a DNA sequence. EcoRII is a restriction enzyme that searches for the sequence ---CCWGG--- where W could be either A or T, and splices it open like ---NN___CCWGGNN--- where another DNA strand can be spliced into the underscored region.

Given a DNA sequence to be spliced using EcoRII and a DNA strand to be spliced in afterward, what is the complement of the DNA sequence resulting from the splicing process?
