Deoxyribonucleic acid (DNA) is a molecule found A DNA sequence is a string of ATCG characters representing possible nucleotide bases of which there are four: adenine (A), cytosine (C), guanine (G), and thymine (T). The complement of a DNA sequence is obtained by substituting each base with its base pair (A and T are one pair. C and G is the other). Together the two strands containing the sequence form a DNA molecule.
